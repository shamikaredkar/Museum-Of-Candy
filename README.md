### Purpose:

The Museum of Candy is a web-based project that showcases a vibrant and interactive virtual tour of a candy-themed museum. This project aims to bring to life the whimsical and delightful world of candy through a series of playful and creative web pages. With emphasis on visual appeal and ease of use, this project is designed to engage users of all ages in the sweet and colourful universe of confections.

---

### Features:

> A fixed-top navigation bar that becomes transparent as the user scrolls.
> 

> An engaging homepage with a dynamic typographic design that spells out "Museum of Candy" in an eye-catching manner.
> 

> Interactive sections showcasing candy-themed art installations with immersive descriptions.
>

> Responsive design ensuring a seamless experience across various devices and screen sizes.
>

> Hover effects and animations that enhance the user experience.
>

> Call-to-action links that guide users through the virtual tour.
> 
---

### Technology Stack:

HTML, CSS, External Fonts (Google Fonts), jQuery, Bootstrap 4
